Padma Shri Gowri Ishwaran

Chief Mentor, ZOE International School

In 2004, the Government of India awarded Gowri Ishwaran the 4th highest civilian honour – the Padma Shri - for her contributions to the Indian Educational sector. She has taught at prestigious schools across India such as St. Michael's School, Patna, and Delhi Public School and Springdales School, Delhi. She is the founder of the prestigious Sanskriti School, Vice-Chairperson of the Global Education & Leadership Foundation (GELF), advisor to the Shiv Nadar Foundation and has supported many education initiatives and social causes over the years.

As Chief Mentor, she brings a wealth of experience and wisdom to ZOE International School.
